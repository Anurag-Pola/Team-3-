About Us


We are a group of technical individuals who are working towards building an effective and simple online marketplace for facilitating and providing a platform for various FPO's to advertise their produce and showing them to potential consumers. We believe in supporting our local farmers and providing farm fresh products at best rates. We at Jaljeevika are all about quality and supporting our local farmers.

Our Mission


We constantly strive to bring the best and freshest farm produce to our consumers and at the same time encourage our local farmers to register to FPO's for making the best out of their work. Our team has people of great technical knowledge who are constantly working towards making these farmers as well as consumers to reach out to each other and get the best quality farm produce at good prices for a win-win for both

Farmer Producer Organisation


An Farmer Producer Organisation (FPO), formed by a group of farm producers, is a registered body with producers as shareholders in the organisation. It deals with business activities related to the farm produce and it works for the benefit of the member producers and makes sure that no farmer’s hard work goes waste.
